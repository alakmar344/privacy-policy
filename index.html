Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 3, 2026

1. How Data Is Handled

eSAMz prioritizes user privacy through a "Privacy First" architecture. We process your messages in real-time to generate responses and strictly limit data retention.

We do not create permanent user accounts, profiles, or login credentials. You interact with eSAMz anonymously via temporary session identifiers.

2. Session Memory & Cookies

Short-Term Context: To provide a coherent conversation experience, eSAMz uses temporary session memory.

To facilitate this, we use a browser "Cookie" (named esamz_sid). This cookie contains a random session ID that allows our server to recognize you during a single visit.

  • Duration: Session data is retained in volatile memory for the duration of your active use. If you are inactive or close your browser, this data is discarded.
  • Content: This memory may include recent chat context to help the AI understand the flow of conversation.
  • Control: You can disable this cookie at any time via your browser settings, though this will reset the AI's memory of the current conversation.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

Beyond the temporary session context mentioned above, we do not collect or store the following:

  • Permanent chat transcripts or archives
  • User accounts, usernames, or passwords
  • Email addresses or phone numbers
  • Payment information
  • Voice recordings or audio data
  • Biometric identifiers

4. System Logs

Minimal technical logs are maintained strictly for security, debugging, and abuse prevention. These logs may include request timestamps and response codes. Access logs are retained for no longer than 48 hours and are then permanently deleted.

5. Local Browser Storage

We may store interface preferences locally on your device (using LocalStorage). This data never leaves your device and can be deleted at any time using your browser's "Clear History" or "Clear Site Data" controls.

6. Third Party Services

  • AI Inference: We use third-party AI providers (e.g., Sarvam AI) to process your messages and generate responses. Your message is sent to them solely to generate the reply and is not stored by them beyond their operational needs.
  • Advertising: We may use advertising networks such as Google AdSense.
  • Infrastructure: Content delivery networks (CDNs) may be used to serve static assets.

Third party services operate under their own independent privacy policies.

7. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area, eSAMz complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

  • Lawful basis: Legitimate interest (providing the requested service) and user consent (via use of the service).
  • Right to access: You can view the data processed during your session.
  • Right to rectification: You can correct information by simply chatting with the AI.
  • Right to erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Since we do not permanently store data, clearing your browser cookies or closing the session effectively erases your data from our systems immediately.
  • Right to data portability: Not applicable, as no long-term personal data store is maintained.

8. CCPA and CPRA Compliance

For California residents, eSAMz does not sell, rent, or share personal information.

  • No personal data sale
  • No profiling for marketing purposes
  • No cross-site behavioral tracking

You may request disclosure or deletion of any applicable data by clearing your local browser data or contacting us.

9. Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data is transmitted over HTTPS.
  • API Protection: Server-side API keys are protected and never exposed to the client.
  • No Accounts: We eliminate the risk of password breaches by not having user accounts.
  • Minimal Retention: Data is discarded as soon as it is no longer needed for the immediate session.

10. Children’s Privacy

eSAMz is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

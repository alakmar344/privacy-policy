Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 3, 2026

1. How Data Is Handled

No server side chat storage.

eSAMz does not store chat messages or conversation content on its servers. Messages are transmitted only to generate a response and are discarded immediately after processing.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • Chat transcripts
  • User accounts or passwords
  • Email addresses or phone numbers
  • Payment information
  • Voice recordings or audio data
  • Biometric identifiers

3. System Logs

Minimal technical logs are maintained strictly for security and abuse prevention. These logs may include timestamps and response codes and are retained for no longer than 48 hours.

4. Local Browser Storage

Some data such as interface preferences and conversation history may be stored locally in your browser. This data never leaves your device and can be deleted at any time using browser controls.

5. Third Party Services

  • AI inference providers for response generation
  • Advertising networks such as Google AdSense
  • Content delivery networks

Third party services operate under their own privacy policies.

6. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area, eSAMz complies with the General Data Protection Regulation.

  • Lawful basis: legitimate interest and user initiated requests
  • Right to access, rectification, and erasure
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability where applicable

As no personal data is stored server side, most requests can be fulfilled instantly by clearing local browser data.

7. CCPA and CPRA Compliance

For California residents, eSAMz does not sell, rent, or share personal information.

  • No personal data sale
  • No profiling
  • No cross site behavioral tracking

You may request disclosure or deletion of any applicable data by contacting us.

8. Security Measures

  • HTTPS encryption
  • Server side API key protection
  • No account system
  • Minimal retention architecture

9. Children’s Privacy

eSAMz is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

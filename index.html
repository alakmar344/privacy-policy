Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 3, 2026

eSAMz AI is designed with privacy in mind. This Privacy Policy explains what data is processed, where it is stored, and how it is used when you interact with our services.

1. How Your Data Is Handled

No server-side chat storage by eSAMz.

eSAMz does not store your conversation content on our servers. Messages are sent to the AI provider only to generate a response and are not retained by us.

For user convenience, conversation history and settings may be stored locally in your browser using browser storage technologies such as localStorage. This local data never leaves your device unless you actively send a message for processing.

2. Data We Do Not Collect on Our Servers

eSAMz does not intentionally collect or store on its servers:

  • Chat messages or conversation history
  • User accounts or login credentials
  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Financial or payment information
  • Voice recordings or raw audio files
  • Biometric or identity data

3. System Logs

Like most web services, eSAMz maintains minimal technical logs for reliability and security. These logs may include:

  • Request timestamps
  • HTTP status codes
  • General error information

These logs are used strictly for system maintenance and abuse prevention and are retained for a limited period, typically no longer than 24–48 hours.

4. Local Browser Storage

eSAMz may store the following data locally on your device:

  • Conversation history for your convenience
  • Interface preferences and settings
  • Voice configuration choices

This information is stored entirely within your browser and can be cleared at any time by using your browser settings or the in-app clear history option.

5. Third-Party Services

eSAMz relies on third-party services to operate:

  • AI Providers (e.g. Sarvam) — Messages are sent for response generation and handled according to their privacy policies.
  • Advertising Services — Ads may be served by third-party networks such as Google AdSense, which may use cookies or similar technologies.
  • Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) — Used to deliver fonts and scripts efficiently.

We do not control how third-party services process data. Please review their respective privacy policies.

6. API Key Security

All API keys used by eSAMz are stored securely on the server and are never exposed to users. Requests are handled in-memory and are not logged with message content.

7. Security Measures

  • HTTPS encryption for all communications
  • Server-side protection of API credentials
  • No user account system
  • Minimal data retention

8. Your Control and Rights

You control your locally stored data. You may:

  • Clear conversation history at any time
  • Export conversations manually
  • Stop using the service without creating an account

9. Children’s Privacy

eSAMz is not designed specifically for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The “Last Updated” date will reflect the latest revision.

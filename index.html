Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 8, 2024

Your privacy is our absolute priority. eSAMz is built with a zero-storage architecture — we do not store, save, or retain any of your conversations or personal data.

1. Zero Data Storage Policy

eSAMz DOES NOT store any of your data.

We operate on a strict zero-storage principle:

  • No conversation history — Your chats are not saved anywhere
  • No user profiles — We don't create or maintain accounts
  • No message logs — Your questions and responses are processed in real-time and immediately discarded
  • No session data — We don't track your usage across sessions

Every conversation is completely ephemeral. Once you close your browser or refresh the page, everything is gone forever.

2. What We Absolutely Do NOT Store

eSAMz does not collect, store, or retain:

  • ❌ Chat messages or conversation content
  • ❌ Personal information (name, email, phone, address)
  • ❌ User accounts or login credentials
  • ❌ IP addresses or device identifiers
  • ❌ Cookies or tracking data
  • ❌ Browsing history or usage patterns
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Biometric data
  • ❌ Financial information
  • ❌ Any personally identifiable information (PII)

3. System Logs (The Only Data We Keep)

The only data we collect consists of minimal system logs for technical operations:

3.1 What's in Our Logs

  • HTTP status codes (200 OK, 404 Not Found, 500 Internal Server Error)
  • Timestamps of requests
  • General error messages for debugging

3.2 What's NOT in Our Logs

  • ❌ Message content
  • ❌ User identifiers
  • ❌ Personal information
  • ❌ IP addresses
  • ❌ Device information
These logs are automatically deleted after 24-48 hours and are used solely for system maintenance and error tracking.

4. No Dify, No Third-Party Storage

eSAMz is NOT affiliated with Dify or any third-party data platforms.

We do not use:

  • ❌ Dify
  • ❌ External database services
  • ❌ Cloud storage platforms for user data
  • ❌ Analytics services that track users
  • ❌ Advertising networks
  • ❌ Data brokers or aggregators

Your conversations are processed directly through Grok API and are not stored by us or any intermediary service.

5. API Key Handling

Our sarvam API key is:

  • ✅ Stored securely as a server-side environment variable
  • ✅ Never exposed to clients or browsers
  • ✅ Never logged or saved in any files
  • ✅ Processed only in memory during request handling
Your messages are sent to sarvam for processing, but we do not store them. sarvam data handling is governed by their own privacy policy.

6. Security Measures

Even though we don't store your data, we still implement security best practices:

  • ✅ HTTPS encryption for all communications
  • ✅ Secure server-side API key management
  • ✅ No client-side data persistence
  • ✅ Regular security audits
  • ✅ Minimal attack surface (no databases, no user accounts)

7. Your Rights

Since we don't store any of your data, there is:

  • ✅ Nothing to delete (we don't save anything)
  • ✅ Nothing to export (no data exists)
  • ✅ Nothing to correct (no records kept)
  • ✅ No account to deactivate (we don't have accounts)

You maintain complete control simply by using the service — when you leave, everything is already gone.

8. Children's Privacy

eSAMz does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

Since we store no data at all, there is no risk of retaining information from minors.

However, we recommend parental supervision for users under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect:

  • New features or functionality
  • Legal or regulatory requirements
  • Improvements to our privacy practices

Any changes will be posted with an updated "Last Updated" date.

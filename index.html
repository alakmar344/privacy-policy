Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 3, 2025

Your privacy is a fundamental priority at eSAMz. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, protect, and manage information when you access or interact with our service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Chat Logs

We maintain chat logs that may include:

  • message content
  • timestamps
  • usage patterns
  • internal system metadata

These logs are essential for:

  • improving answer accuracy
  • detecting and reducing system errors
  • strengthening safety and abuse-prevention systems
  • ensuring operational stability and performance

1.2 Technical & Non-Personal Data

We may collect general usage information such as:

  • device type
  • browser type
  • performance metrics
  • interaction timing

This information does not identify you personally.

2. What We Do Not Collect

eSAMz does not request or collect:

  • login or account registration
  • email
  • phone number
  • government ID
  • advertising identifiers
  • biometric data

We do not run hidden trackers, third-party analytics tools, or advertising beacons.

We strongly advise users not to share:

  • passwords
  • financial data
  • medical information
  • private identification details

3. How We Use Your Data

All collected information is used solely for:

  • improving the quality and accuracy of responses
  • maintaining platform reliability
  • enhancing security and safety
  • debugging technical issues
  • preventing misuse or harmful activity

We do not:

  • sell user data
  • rent or trade user information
  • share logs with advertisers
  • provide logs to external third parties

4. Data Protection & Security

4.1 Security Standards

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect stored data.

These measures include access controls, secure storage layers, and restricted administrative access.

4.2 Session Management

We use UUID-based session identifiers for user recognition. These identifiers are never shared with third parties under any circumstances.

Your session data is kept completely confidential.

4.3 Access Restrictions

Only one authorized administrator may view internal logs when required for critical security or technical issues. We do not routinely study or analyze your conversations.

5. Data Retention Policy

  • Chat logs may be retained for operational, safety, debugging, and system performance purposes.
  • We do not promise automatic deletion on a fixed schedule, as some system-level logs are maintained by our AI service provider.
  • If you request deletion of your stored conversation data, we will remove all data that we control.
  • System-level logs maintained by infrastructure providers may not be manually deletable, but they are handled under GDPR-compliant processing agreements.
  • We comply with GDPR and CCPA by deleting any conversation data we control upon request. Certain system-level logs may be retained by our AI infrastructure provider (Dify) for security, operational, and regulatory purposes under their own GDPR-compliant data processing policies.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • understand what we collect and why
  • request clarification on our data practices
  • request deletion of any conversation logs that we store
  • ask questions about data safety

You can exercise your right to deletion by contacting us via email.

7. Children's Privacy

eSAMz is not designed for children under 13.

Minors should use the platform only under adult supervision.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect:

  • new service features
  • legal requirements
  • security improvements

Updated versions will be published with revised dates.

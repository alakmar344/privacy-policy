Privacy Policy - eSAMz AI

Last Updated: June 6, 2026

Compliance with Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP), 2023

eSAMz AI is committed to protecting the personal data of its users in accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP), 2023, of India. This section outlines our adherence to the principles and requirements of the DPDP Act, particularly concerning consent, data processing, and data principal rights.

Data Processing and Consent

We process your personal data based on explicit, informed consent obtained through clear affirmative action, or where otherwise permitted by law. Before processing any personal data, you will be presented with a standalone notice detailing:

  • The categories of personal data being processed (e.g., email for account creation, session prompts for response generation, minimal system logs for security).
  • The specific purposes for which your personal data is processed (e.g., generating AI responses, authentication, account management, security, abuse prevention).
  • Your rights as a Data Principal under the DPDP Act, including the right to access, correct, erase, and withdraw consent.
  • The mechanism for exercising these rights and for grievance redressal.
  • Contact information for our Data Protection Officer or designated contact person.

Prominent Consent Notice: For critical interactions such as first visits, Google login, or initiating a chat, a clear consent notice will be displayed, requiring your affirmative action. An example of such a notice is:

"eSAMz processes your session inputs (and email for accounts) solely to generate responses and manage authentication. Data is wiped after 30 minutes. See Privacy Policy for details. Do you consent to this processing? [I Agree] [No, Learn More]"

Your consent will be specific to each purpose and will not be implied by your continued use of the service.

Data Principal Rights under DPDP Act

As a Data Principal, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access and Correction: You have the right to request access to your personal data and to request corrections to any inaccuracies.
  • Right to Erasure: Your session data is automatically erased after 30 minutes of inactivity. For account-related data, you can request erasure by contacting us. We will process such requests in accordance with applicable laws.
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: If you have any concerns or grievances regarding the processing of your personal data, you may contact our designated grievance officer. We are committed to addressing your concerns within a reasonable timeframe.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time. Withdrawal of consent will not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal. To withdraw consent for session data, you can clear your browser cookies. For account-related data, please contact us.

1. How Data Is Handled

eSAMz prioritizes user privacy through a "Privacy First" architecture. We process your messages in real-time to generate responses and strictly limit data retention.

We do not create permanent user accounts or profiles for general usage. You interact with eSAMz largely anonymously via session identifiers.

2. Session Memory & Cookies

Strict 30-Minute Retention: eSAMz v9.1 uses a temporary session cookie (esamz_sid) to manage your active conversation.

  • Short-Term Memory: This cookie and the associated server memory are strictly set to expire after 30 minutes of inactivity.
  • Local Storage: Your chat history is saved securely on your device. Even if the active session memory clears, you can still view and reference your past conversations.
  • Automatic Wipe: If you stop interacting with the chat for 30 minutes, the server automatically wipes the memory of that conversation. When you return, you will start fresh with a new session ID.
  • No Tracking: This cookie is only used to link your current messages together. It is not used for cross-site tracking or advertising profiling.

3. Data We Collect and Do Not Collect

We minimize data collection to the absolute essentials:

Data We Collect:

  • Email Addresses: We collect and store email addresses when users create or sign in to an account on eSAMz AI. This email is used for authentication, account management, and security purposes.
    • Exceptions:
      1. If you purchase a Premium license, your email may also be used to verify your license or key.
      2. If you voluntarily contact us for support or partnerships, we retain that correspondence.
  • Payment Information: We do not store credit card or payment details. Payments are processed by third-party providers. We store a payment reference ID to verify subscription status.
  • Session Prompts: Your input prompts during a session are processed in real-time to generate responses and are subject to the 30-minute retention policy.

Data We Do Not Collect:

  • Permanent Transcripts: We do not maintain permanent, long-term archives of your conversations once they are cleared from active memory (30 mins limit).
  • Voice Data: We do not store voice recordings. Voice input is processed locally or via your browser\'s speech recognition service.

4. System Logs

Minimal technical logs are maintained strictly for security, debugging, and abuse prevention. These logs may include request timestamps and response codes. Access logs are retained for short periods (typically 48 hours) and are then deleted.

5. Local Browser Storage

We store your chat history locally on your device (using localStorage). This ensures you have immediate access to past conversations while you are viewing the page, without us needing to permanently warehouse them on our servers.

6. Third Party Services

  • AI Inference: We use third-party AI providers (e.g., Model Providers) to generate responses. The processing of your session prompts by these providers is governed by their respective privacy policies and is subject to our 30-minute retention policy.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand website traffic trends (e.g., "How many users visited today?"). This service collects anonymous usage data and may use its own cookies. You will be presented with a cookie consent banner for non-essential cookies.
  • Infrastructure: We use Vercel and Upstash Redis for hosting and caching.

Third-party services operate under their own independent privacy policies. We ensure that our agreements with these third parties align with our commitment to data protection.

7. Children’s Privacy

eSAMz AI is not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 18 without verifiable parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information as quickly as possible. Our Terms of Service reflect this age restriction.
